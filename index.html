OUR MISSION

In our country, the percentage of motivated students from low-income background, who on the verge of dropping out, is quite high. So, serving at the grassroots level by empowering those underprivileged children and communities via education is very important. Uttoroner Disha Foundation aims to improve the quality of education and knowledge in our society through financial assistance (such as scholarships), career counseling, and walking through many different available opportunities in India and abroad. Our mission is to create a positive impact on the lives of needy meritorious students, fostering respect for their dignity, fairness, and equity, and improving the quality of life.

OUR VISSION

The ultimate objective of Uttoroner Disha FoundatioN is to enable our society to thrive and care for great education for every individual, regardless of any social boundaries or economic obstacles. specially for those underprivileged students in rural India and allowing them to reach their full potential

OUR OBJECTIVE

We support orphan, poor, helpless meritorious students. We Provide them monetary support by scholarship, tuition fees, admission fees. We arrange study materials for those students Guide them for higher study in India & Abroad.
